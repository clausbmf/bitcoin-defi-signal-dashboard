🐋 Bitcoin Humpback Whale Tracker

Transforming Bitcoin whale activity into actionable DeFi signals that help capital work on Bitcoin L1.

Bitcoin Humpback Whale Tracker is a real-time dashboard that monitors large Bitcoin transactions and converts whale movements into market insights and DeFi signals.

The tool detects whale transactions above 100 BTC, classifies them by size, and provides insights into market pressure and potential capital allocation strategies.

Leave the page open with sound enabled to hear whale activity while the dashboard builds a live history of large Bitcoin transfers.

🚀 Features
🐋 Real-Time Whale Detection

Detects Bitcoin transactions above 100 BTC and displays them in a live dashboard.

🐬 Whale Size Classification

Transactions are categorized by size:

🐬 Dolphin: 100–499 BTC

🐋 Whale: 500–999 BTC

🐋 Humpback Whale: 1000–4999 BTC

🐳 Blue Whale: 5000+ BTC

🔊 Audio Alerts

Different whale sizes trigger different sound alerts.
Users can toggle sound ON / OFF.

🌊 Whale Wave Detection

Detects clusters of whale transactions happening within a short time window.

📊 Whale Market Signal

Analyzes whale flows to identify:

accumulation signals

potential sell pressure

🕰 Dormant Whale Detection

Highlights when long-inactive Bitcoin suddenly moves.

📈 Whale Activity Insights

Shows patterns of whale activity over time.

💰 The DeFi Signal

Includes a Bitcoin DeFi strategy simulator showing how BTC could be allocated across Bitcoin ecosystems to generate yield.

🏆 Whale of the Day

Displays the largest whale transaction detected in the last 24 hours.

🔍 On-Chain Transparency

All whale alerts are derived from public Bitcoin blockchain data and can be verified through blockchain explorers such as:

mempool.space

blockstream.info
